Research Framework

Softmode investigates clay as an input modality, a functional material system, and an embodied output medium for human-AI interaction.

INPUT

Semantic Interaction Through Shape

Exploring how shape and gesture encode human intent and function as nonverbal input to AI systems.

MATERIAL

Advanced Clay-Based Material Systems

Investigating self-healing, conductive, and embeddable materials for tangible interaction and computation.

OUTPUT

Simulated Shape-Changing Material Output

Using haptic simulation to explore how hypothetical shape-changing clay could physically represent AI-generated forms.
